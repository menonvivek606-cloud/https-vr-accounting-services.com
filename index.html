Corporate Filings

At Vivek Ramadas & Co, we provide end-to-end support for corporate filings, ensuring timely and accurate compliance with statutory and regulatory requirements. Our services cover filings with the Registrar of Companies (ROC) and other authorities, helping businesses stay compliant and avoid penalties. We assist with preparation and filing of annual returns, financial statements, and other mandatory disclosures, along with event-based filings such as changes in directors, share capital, and registered office. With a structured and detail-oriented approach, we ensure that all filings are completed efficiently and in accordance with applicable laws. Our focus is to simplify compliance, reduce administrative burden, and allow our clients to concentrate on their core business operations.
